Barney's Day Off is a multiplayer map for HALF-LIFE. It incorporates many techniques that stretch the GoldSource Engine to its limits.

Barney's Day Off is currently in an alpha testing stage. To launch the game, you must have the proprietary game HALF-LIFE and it's GoldSource technologies.


Drag and drop the BSP file into the valve/maps directory in your HALF-LIFE Installation folder.
